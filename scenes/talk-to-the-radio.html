Signal: 104.5 "The Echo"

STATUS: COMMUNICATION | SANITY: 90%

You lift the WakiTalki to your mouth. The static is thick, like trying to shout through a wet cloth.

YOU:

“What is going on?, Who are you, did you send out the transmission.”

The radio crackles again.

RADIO (104.5):

“You shouldn't have answered. They know you're here now.”

“They”?
You look back down the path, but the fog has thickened behind you. You can't even see the path anymore.

RADIO (104.5 - URGENT):

"I can't talk. They’re moving. They’re using the echo. Go. Go now. Don't look at the lights, and *don't stop* until you reach the tower!"
