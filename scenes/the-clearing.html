The trees here are unnaturally tall, their trunks twisting upward like they’re trying to escape something below. Their branches interlock so tightly that the daylight fades to a dim grey haze. Fog crawls along the forest floor, weaving between roots like it has a mind of its own

Somewhere far ahead, you hear a faint clatter "metal on stone, or maybe bone on bark". No one comes out here anymore. No one wants to. You start looking around,

You scan the area. To your left, an old stump is covered in leaves, but there is glowing moss.
