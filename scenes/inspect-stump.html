Investigation: Anomaly 01

SANITY: 95% (Unstable)

You kneel in the mud, your boots sinking slightly. Up close, the moss isn’t just glowing it’s pulsing, a slow, rhythmic beat, like it has a heartbeat of its own. It hasn’t grown randomly; someone or something has carefully cultivated it into a deliberate, unnerving shape.

Your fingers tremble as you pull out your UV light. A beam cuts through the fog, striking the bark. The moss reacts immediately, illuminating patterns that weren’t visible before: intricate symbols, almost like a language carved in living green.

A shiver runs down your spine. Whoever or whatever did this knew you would see it. It hasn't grown randomly. It has been cultivated into a shape.

Under the UV beam, a symbol burns bright green against the dark wood. At first glance, it resembles a pine tree but inverted. Its roots claw toward the sky, while its branches dig deep into the earth, twisting unnaturally into the mud.

The sight makes your stomach tighten. It’s as if the forest itself is watching, marking its territory—or issuing a warning.
Something about this place is alive… and it knows you’re here.
