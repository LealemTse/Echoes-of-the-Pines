Location: Tower 4

OBJECTIVE: INVESTIGATE RANGER STATION

As you approach the tower thing radio starts to beep.

RADIO (104.5):

“Where are, are you there yet?”

You:

“Ya, I'm getting in the tower, I run as fast as I can”

The door creaks open. The tower smells of old paper. Maps cover every inch of the walls, obscured by frantic red marker lines.

A handwritten note is pinned to the center of the main map with a knife.

“They aren't lost.
They are being kept. The fog uses the metal as a conductor.
If you find the Anchor, DO NOT TAKE IT OUT yet.
Place it on the map. Align the inscription with the Ley Lines.”

You get confused, then you look down. A loose floorboard near the window has been pried up. Something silver glints in the darkness beneath it.

ITEM ACQUIRED: SILVER LOCKET
Inscription: 44°N - The Singing Tree

It's heavy. As soon as you touch it, the static gets louder. The radio clicks on.

RADIO (104.5):

“You found it. Okay, listen. That Ranger wasn't crazy. He was trying to map the Perimeter.”

You:

“What? What is the Perimeter?”

RADIO (104.5):

“Bring the locket to the wall map.”

You hold the locket up to the wall map, it a matching for the "Singing Tree" inscription to a red circle on the paper.

When the silver touches the paper, the red marker lines seem to shift. You realize the pattern isn't random. The red circles form a Pentagon around the valley.

Tower 4 isn't a watchtower. It's a fence post. And the locket is a battery.

RADIO (104.5):

“If you remove that locket, you break the circuit. The fog will expand. But if you leave it... the things trapped inside stay trapped. Including the missing people.”

RADIO (104.5):

“You don't know it, but you're now the gate kipper of the forest”
