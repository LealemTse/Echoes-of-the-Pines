Location: Tower 4

OBJECTIVE: LOCATE LOCKET

The door creaks open. The air inside is stale, smelling of old paper and dust. It looks like the ranger just stepped out for a break... in 1999.

A coffee mug sits on the desk, the liquid inside evaporated to a black stain. Maps of the valley cover the walls, covered in frantic red marker lines.

You open the top drawer. There, resting on a pile of unpaid bills, is the Silver Locket.

You reach for it, but the radio crackles to life. This time, the voice is crystal clear.

RADIO (104.5):

"Wait. Check the inscription on the back."

The silver is warm. Scratched into the back are the words:

"For Emily. So she never gets lost in the Static."

RADIO (104.5):

"That locket is an anchor. If you take it out of the valley, the fog... it gets loose. Your client knows that."

You hold the item. $500 payout if you take it. But the voice sounds terrified.
